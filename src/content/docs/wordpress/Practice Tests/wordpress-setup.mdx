## Test 3

[AP Psychology Test - Abnormal Behavior](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe7n1RxH0lGsmuRDr9vcbZdMjZILcQ56BORdQv5w50ec0nLIQ/viewform?usp=sf_link) is designed to help students prepare for the Advanced Placement (AP) Psychology exam. It covers key concepts and topics from the course, including research methods, biological bases of behavior, cognition, development, personality, abnormal psychology, and social psychology. By taking this practice test, students can assess their knowledge, identify areas for improvement, and get familiar with the types of questions they will encounter on the actual exam. This test serves as a valuable tool for reinforcing key psychological concepts and boosting confidence in preparation for the AP Psychology exam.
